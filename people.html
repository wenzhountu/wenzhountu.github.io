Principal Investigator

Wen Zhou (周文)

Dr. Wen Zhou is an Assistant Professor in the School of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Nanyang Technological University. She received her bachelor's (2017) and master's (2020) degrees from Tsinghua University, and her PhD in Civil Engineering (2023) from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Following her doctoral studies, she worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Delft University of Technology (2023-2025).
