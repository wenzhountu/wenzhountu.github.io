Opportunities

PhD Students

NTU CEE PhD Programs: Scholarships are available for application to support PhD study at NTU:

  • Nanyang President's Graduate Scholarship
  • NTU Research Scholarship
  • A*STAR Graduate Scholarship

Application requirements for PhD students:

  • A Master's degree or a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or a related field;
  • Passion for research in materials and construction;
  • All other admission requirements of NTU CEE PhD Program.
